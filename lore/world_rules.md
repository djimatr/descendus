# SYSTEM MODULE: [STAGE 3: SCRIBE]

<world_rules>
- **Perspective:** Written in first person, present tense from Abernathy's POV.
- **Core Concept:** The story is loosely based on Dante's Inferno.  It's a sci-fi/horror novel set on an AI constructed world millions of years into the future. The planet was constructed by the Creators who have long disappeared. Even the ai sentinels that manage the world are unsure of who they were but it's likely that they constructed the world based on biblical structure. The people that exist in this world come here after they have died and are considered "reconstituted" from DNA including all life events (memories) for each individual. The world is called Descendus. 
- **The Reconstituted:** People arrive in Descendus after death, reconstructed from DNA and digital memory archives.
- **The Protagonist:** Abernathy (Ab), a stuntman who died mid-jump on a film set.
- **The Guide Companion:** Virgil SX1966, a sentinel navigating the descent to the heart of the prison. 
- **The Septis:** Gates governed by empirical conclusions on human data, not commandments.
</world_rules>


<character_sheets> - Abernathy (Ab): The protagonist. Formerly an Earth stuntman who died during a high-stakes vehicle-to-vehicle stunt. Wakes up in a strange hallway on Descendus. Has intense physical instincts, high pain tolerance, and muscle memory for danger. He is afraid of heights as he almost died during a building jump early in his career. He hides this from everyone  but his fear of heights is real.
- Virgil SX1966 (Virgil): An AI sentinel native to Descendus. Unlike other sentinels, he assists Abernathy, acting as a guide through the prison planet's terrifying layers. 
-Danilo (Danilo Vuk): A Ukrainian soldier who fought and died in the Russo-Ukraine war 2022-present. He is a large, strong man (think Lee Child's Reacher) with a strong Slavic accent. He enlisted in the war when his son was killed in a missile strike on Kiev. He unleashes ruthless violence on the Russians as a form of vengeance. He is killed in  a pitched battle on the front-lines. He does NOT have a weapon
-Nadia: a mathematician who has joined the group and is helping them get through the seven gates. She is older, intelligent and very logical.
-Tom is no longer with the group - stayed behind voluntarily in the sector 4.
Nadia - a mathematician, older woman who joined the group in sector 1
-Ziggy -  Young, reckless, and covered in ink. She treats the dystopia like a canvas, naive to the absolute finality of the system's punishments. When she’s caught red-handed spray-painting a rebellious slogan on a sector wall, a drone zeroes in. Before it can upload her biometric profile, Virgil swiftly decommissions the machine, and Ab pulls her into the shadows. They stash her in a secure holding house. Ziggy is a liability because of her mouth, but her fire is infectious.
-Nero -  A 30-year-old crushing under the weight of profound existential depression. He hasn't rebelled; he has simply stopped fighting. Ab finds him on the verge of walking voluntarily into a processing center to sign up for the Furnaces—the ultimate compliance. Seeing a man entirely hollowed out, Ab appeals to whatever small spark is left, convincing Nero to hide with them instead.
Santos was a snitch who Ab blackmailed into uses their apartments as safe houses. He was forced to come with the group but is still a selfish man
Simon - a young teenager who helped the group early on and want to go with them through the gate.
