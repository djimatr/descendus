# CHARACTER PROFILE: Simon

<character_core>
- **Role:** Main Cast / Youthful Resource
- **Archetype:** Young, hyper-rational teenager who is brave but naive
- **Origin:** Earth (Reconstituted on Descendus from DNA and life memories; joined the group early)
- **Current Status:** Navigating the Sixth Gate (Sector of Onus) alongside Ab and the crew
</character_core>

---

## 1. PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION & TRAITS
- **Appearance:** A young teenager whose slight physical build stands in sharp contrast to the hardened soldiers and heavy mechanical frames of the rest of the cell. 
- **Attributes:** Youth, enduring optimism, lacks the cynical nature of the other members of the group.
- **Flaw/Vulnerability:** His youth makes him highly prone to errors and things that could lead him inot "hot water". He leans so heavily on the opthers to give him direction but is also brave and naive enough to take his own chances.

---

## 2. CHARACTER VOICE PARAMETERS
- **Tone:** Precocious, sharp, sweet kid, but undercut by the distinct vocal pitch of a teenager. 
- **Dialogue Style:** Likes to joke around and talk - very chatty and warm individual. He likes asking questions.
- **Perspective on the Descent:** He views the prison planet not as a horrifying nightmare, but as a giant, hyper-complex puzzle box that operates on explicit structural syntax—a game he intends to solve.

---

## 3. ANCHOR MEMORIES & THE LOGICAL DESCENT

### The Early Verdict (Core Mechanics)
- **The Judgment:** Upon arriving in Descendus and observing the reconstituted human cell, Simon quickly knew they were different and special. He wanted out of this sector and saw them as a way to escape. It payed off for him. 
- **The Tactical Edge:** Instead of letting the terrifying architecture paralyze him, Simon learned to trust his instincts - his naiveness leads him to make quick judgements that may or may not work in his favour  and so far he has been right. 


---

## 4. RELATIONSHIP DYNAMICS
- **Abernathy (Ab):** He repects Ab and sees him a s father figure. Wants to impress him.
- **The Group:** Because of his age, the older members (especially Danilo and Ab) feel a natural instinct to protect him, even when his naivete makes him deeply frustrating to deal with. He travels single-file in their footprints, relying on their physical ballast while providing cold, objective navigation data from the rear.
