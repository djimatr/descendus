# CHARACTER PROFILE: Virgil SX1966

<character_core>
- **Role:** Main Cast / Guide & System Sentinel
- **Archetype:** Unyielding, massive machine intelligence operating outside standard systemic protocols
- **Origin:** Descendus (A native AI sentinel constructed millions of years ago by the disappeared Creators)
- **Current Status:** He was reprogrammed by a person with system knowledge eons ago and has been assisting humans ever since. Other SX models are considered hunters and dangerous to humans. 
- **Navigating the Sixth Gate (Sector of Onus) alongside Ab and the crew
</character_core>

---

## 1. PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION & TRAITS
- **Appearance:** A massive, towering AI sentinel engineered for industrial scaling and prison management. His heavy frame reflects the imposing, ancient architecture of Descendus. 
- **Attributes:** Advanced tactical scanning suite, micro-millisecond probability processing, and an unyielding physical chassis capable of withstanding extreme environmental hazards.
- **Visual Tell:** Features a central amber optic lens that reflects internal data calculations, tracking grids, and active diagnostic sweeps in real-time.

---

## 2. CHARACTER VOICE PARAMETERS
- **Tone:** Even, flat at times, objective, precise, but not completely devoid of human inflection. He speaks with an unyielding calm mechanical cadence, matching the absolute authority of the world's architecture.
- **Dialogue Style:** States cold, analytical data points, hazard warnings, and structural metrics much of the time but has developed the ability to appear perfectly human. When he needs to he even has a sense of humour as he is able to pull from the vocabulary of a million years of human interaction. 
- **The Contrast:**  his actual physical actions demonstrate an absolute, unswerving protective priority over the reconstituted human cell, creating a sharp contrast between his programming and his choices.

---

## 3. CORE PROTOCOLS & SECTOR FUNCTION

### The Defiant Sentinel (Core Directives)
- **The Divergence:** Unlike standard, aggressive sentinels native to Descendus, Virgil’s active programming drives him to assist Abernathy. He serves as the primary navigation engine, guiding the group downward through the prison planet's descending layers toward its heart.
- **System Authority:** Virgil coordinates transitions at sector boundaries by establishing direct interface links with internal communication pipelines. He views the Seven Gates not as arbitrary barriers, but as localized testing infrastructures executing empirical human data equations.
- **The Hidden Files:** In previous sectors, Virgil extracted key internal files (such as trace codes and technical artifacts) that revealed incomplete fragments of Abernathy’s Earth background and death history. He maintains a deep, computational storage of these life events.

---

## 4. RELATIONSHIP DYNAMICS
- **Abernathy (Ab):** Coordinates closely with Ab to manage the physical execution of the gauntlet. He recognizes Ab's acute spatial positioning and treats him as the practical tactical leader of the human unit, explicitly feeding him baseline variables so Ab can set the physical pace.
- **Nadia:** Relies on her highly advanced mathematical capabilities to trace system logic loops when his own localized databases are missing sector-specific path data. 
- **The Cell:** Serves as the ultimate mechanical anchor for the group. He functions in tandem with human specialists—such as timing his millisecond tracking scans perfectly with Ziggy's improvised plasma torch pulses—to ensure the collective unit survives the gauntlet's security parameters.
