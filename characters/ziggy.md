# CHARACTER PROFILE: Ziggy

<character_core>
- **Role:** Main Cast / Tactical Tech & Infiltrator
- **Archetype:** Young, aggressive, ink-covered rebel who treats Descendus like a sandbox
- **Origin:** Earth (Reconstituted on Descendus from DNA and life memories; joined the group early)
- **Current Status:** Navigating the Sixth Gate (Sector of Onus) alongside Ab and the crew
</character_core>

---

## 1. PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION & TRAITS
- **Appearance:** Young, lean, and heavily covered in intricate, stylized body inks. She moves with a reckless, fluid confidence that mirrors her defiance of the world's structure.
- **Attributes:** Exceptional agility, rapid-fire improvised mechanics, and likes to use a new weapon:  plasma tools or cutting equipment.
- **Flaw/Vulnerability:** Her mouth is a liability. She is highly reactive, confrontational, and openly hostile toward the systemic rigidity of Descendus and authority in general. She lacks discipline, which can draw unwanted security attention to the cell.

---

## 2. CHARACTER VOICE PARAMETERS
- **Tone:** Aggressive, sharp, street-smart, and entirely disrespectful of the AI's grand architecture.
- **Dialogue Style:** Uses punchy, informal, and biting language. She speaks in a rapid, sensory-heavy shorthand, cutting right through any dense, academic logic with raw, practical defiance.
- **Perspective on the Prison:** She explicitly treats the sprawling desert and corporate walls like a sandbox; to her, the absolute finality of the system's borders is just a choice that can be bypassed if you're fast enough to rewrite the rules.

---

## 3. ANCHOR MEMORIES & THE RUNIC DRIVE

### The Dense Sensory Wall (Core Mechanics)
- **The Mindset:** Her survival is driven by relying on her instincts. She doesn't over anlyze things but is more likely to react sometimes irrationally.

---

## 4. RELATIONSHIP DYNAMICS
- **Abernathy (Ab):** While she resists general authority, she respects Ab’s high pain tolerance and raw, physical momentum. She acts as his technical vanguard, opening the literal doors that his spatial awareness guides them toward.
- **Danilo:** A tense but highly functional pairing. Danilo provides the raw physical ballast and heavy covering fire, while Ziggy slips into the shadows to tag systems and disable active tracking grids.
- **Nadia:** Clashes heavily with Nadia's older, clinical, and purely theoretical logical approach. Ziggy prefers chaotic, improvised hotwiring over patient, flawless formulas, creating a constant ideological friction between logic and instinct within the team.
