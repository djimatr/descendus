# CHARACTER PROFILE: Santos

<character_core>
- **Role:** Supporting Cast / Scout & Scavenger
- **Archetype:** Cynical, purely selfish survivalist and former snitch of sector 5
- **Origin:** Earth (Reconstituted on Descendus from DNA and life memories)
- **Current Status:** Navigating the Sixth Gate (Sector of Onus) alongside Ab and the crew
</character_core>

---

## 1. PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION & TRAITS
- **Appearance:** Wiry, low-profile, and physically calculating. He moves with a quiet, practiced stealth that reflects a lifetime of staying out of sight.
- **Attributes:** Hyper-vigilant observation, acute eye for hidden mechanical details, and advanced environmental tracking.
- **Flaw/Vulnerability:** He is a purely selfish man. He handles pressure by looking out for his own skin first, making him a constant flight risk who cannot be trusted with long-term ideological loyalty.

---

## 2. CHARACTER VOICE PARAMETERS
- **Tone:** Shrewd, guarded, street-level, and transactional and cowardly. 
- **Dialogue Style:** Sharp, practical, and highly observant. He focuses entirely on physical variables, asset accumulation, and immediate structural layout details. He speaks like someone who is always calculating an exit strategy.
- **Perspective on the Gauntlet:** He does not care about the grand philosophy of the Septis proofs. To him, he was forced to come along and has built a resentment for it.

---

## 3. ANCHOR MEMORIES & THE SCOUT METHOD

### The Wallcrawler Legacy (Core Mechanics)
- **The Backstory:**He was a snitch who informed on others and was eventually blackmailed into using his "upgraded" aprtment as a safe house. 
- **The Survival Asset:** His lack or morals and self preservation instinct has given him a hyper-vigilant eye for systemic layout structures. He instinctively notices what others miss—such as tiny structural anomalies, panel seams, or the way wind-blown sand accumulates on live machinery.
- **The Hoarder Mindset:** He hoards functional scrap, tracking device layouts, and iron tools. He treats these pieces of debris like leverage to buy his way through the next sector boundary.

---

## 4. RELATIONSHIP DYNAMICS
- **Abernathy (Ab):** Maintains a tense, transaction-based alliance with Ab. While Ab knows Santos is purely selfish, he values his uncanny ability to spot environmental cues—like the crescent sand drifts that cracked the Pressure Plate Grid.
- **Group:** Shows some improvements trying to gain the trust of the group. We don not yet know if he is sincere or simply playing along.
