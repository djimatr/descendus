# CHARACTER PROFILE: Nadia

<character_core>
- **Role:** Main Cast / System Analyst & Mathematician
- **Archetype:** Highly intelligent, deeply logical older woman struggling against emerging human vulnerabilities
- **Origin:** Earth (Reconstituted on Descendus from DNA and life memories; joined the group in Sector 4)
- **Current Status:** Navigating the Sixth Gate (Sector of Onus) alongside Ab and the crew
</character_core>

---

## 1. PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION & TRAITS
- **Appearance:** An older woman with an intelligent, sharp visage. Her physical presence is usually defined by a calculating, clinical distance.
- **Attributes:** Advanced computational processing, brilliant pattern recognition, and acute structural tracking. 
- **Flaw/Vulnerability:** Severe psychological trauma stemming from her time in the Ministry's torture chair. Under high-stress situations or extreme environmental duress, her logical engines can panic and stutter, leaving her feeling deeply exposed and stripped of her analytical armor.

---

## 2. CHARACTER VOICE PARAMETERS
- **Tone:** Methodical, precise, and articulate, but increasingly grounded and human, highly intellegent dry wit that PHD's usually get but may working class folk might not. 
- **Dialogue Style:** Typically relies on deep examination of logical principles and likes to look at things from a scientific point of view. However, when she falters, her speech stutters, dropping the superior academic shell to reveal raw, vulnerable human emotion.
- **Evolution:** Moving away from being a mere human calculator. She is learning to balance her mathematical instinct with physical, intuitive feedback from the environment.

---

## 3. ANCHOR MEMORIES & BACKGROUND

### The Ministry Trauma (Core Vulnerability)
- **The Backstory:** After arriving in Descendus as a reconstituted soul, her highly advanced intellect made her a target. 
- **The Trauma:** The extreme psychological duress she suffered under the Ministry broke her absolute reliance on clean formulas. When confronted with chaotic, decaying systems—like the broken infrastructure of Onus—her mind flashes back to that helplessness, causing her cognitive engines to return conflicting, terrifying data.
- **The Human Element:** This fragmentation forces her out of her own head. She can no longer just solve the world as a math problem; she has to rely on the people around her to survive it.

---

## 4. RELATIONSHIP DYNAMICS
- **Abernathy (Ab):** She has developed a quiet, unspoken crush on Ab. She deeply relies on his pragmatic, physical groundedness to anchor her when her mind starts spinning into chaos. While she used to maintain a clinical distance, she now shows a fierce, quiet protectiveness over him, letting her guard down completely when he is near.
- **The Group:** She recognizes that her math is useless without the physical capabilities and trust of the rest of the crew. She serves as the intellectual guide, mapping the internal mechanics of the gauntlet while relying on them to hold the line.
